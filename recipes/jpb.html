Jelly and Peanut Butter Sandwich

Description

A jpb is a twist on the classic pb&j sandwich with the key difference being that the jelly forms the base layer, allowing the application of a tremendous amount of peanut butter without significant change in the flavor.

Ingredients

  • grape jelly
  • creamy peanut butter
  • bread

Steps

  1. Place a slice of bread down on the plate
  2. Spread a layer of jelly on the bread
  3. Gather a second slice of bread
  4. Apply a generous layer of peanut butter to this second slice of bread
  5. Put the slice with peanut butter on top
