Pupusas

Description

Pupusas are a delicious El Salvadorian dish (also popular in Honduras) that is so versatile that they can be combined with almost anything. There are a variety of ways they can be made, so you can have a different experience every time you make them! This recipe will be for pupusas de queso.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of nixtamalized flour
  • 1 and 1/2 cups of warm water (or more if necessary)
  • 1 tsp of salt
  • 1 cup of fresh, shredded cheese
  • 1/4 cup of vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Get a bowl and mix the water, salt, and flour to form a dough
  2. Take some of the dough and form it into a thick tortilla
  3. Add cheese to the middle of the tortilla and then surround it with the dough to make a ball shape
  4. Flatten the the ball back out to form a tortilla once again (about 1/4 inch thick)
  5. Repeat this process until you finish the dough
  6. Rub some oil on both sides of the pupusas with your finger and place them in the pan
  7. Heat the pan (non-stick recommended) to a medium-high level of heat
  8. Cook for about two minutes on each side (or until both sides are golden)
