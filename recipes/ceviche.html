Ceviche

Description

Ceviche is a delicious dish that can be used as an appetizer or as the main entree. It is perfect as a meal that seems fancy but is not too difficult to make. This recipe will focus on shrimp ceviche.

Steps

  1. Peel and prepare about 1 pound of shrimp
  2. Prepare a saucepan on the stove for the shrimp as well as a bowl of ice water next to the stove
  3. Cook the shrimp in boiling water for about 1 minute, then immediately transfer them over to the bowl of ice for about 5 minutes
  4. Chop the shrimp into small pieces and then add them to a glass bowl. Add 1 cup of lime juice, 1/2 cup of lemon juice, and 2 tablespoons of orange juice. Also add in 2 chopped shallots
  5. Cover the bowl and set in the fridge for about an hour
  6. Chop the other vegetables and mix them all together with the shrimp
  7. Serve with tortilla chips
